Lissa Aguilar's Article


The concept of Wireframing opens up a new approch to the User Experience design. I was not familiar with this technique prior to taking the class, however, reading the article by Lissa Aguilar deepened my understanding of web development as a complicated and lengthy process.

I believe, wireframing can be a useful and time-efficient tool used when building a web page. Not only does wireframing take into considerations the tastes and preferences of the clients, but it also allow for better visual representation of ideas and its practical implementation.

To build the recipe website, I decided to use wireframe.cc to create a visual map of my website.
